To celebrate the amazing 1 year we have spent together, I have made a game!!!
It's a 2048 variation with a slight twist - each number corresponds to an event/stage of our relationship.
The lower numbers correspond to earlier events/stages, and the higher numbers the later.
And instead of the number a picture is displayed!! You need to guess what it corresponds to :).

How to play: YOU KNOW HOW TO PLAY 2048 RIGHT!!! If you don't, use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same image touch, they merge into one!
